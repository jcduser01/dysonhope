Privacy Policy

Dyson Hope — dysonhope.com — Last updated: June 2026

What this site is

dysonhope.com is the official artist website for Dyson Hope, an electronic music producer and DJ based in Austin, Texas. This policy explains what data is collected when you visit, and how it is used.

Analytics

This site uses Google Analytics 4 (GA4) via Google Tag Manager (GTM-5T87PLM6) to understand how visitors engage with the music and content. Analytics only loads if you click Accept on the cookie consent bar.

When analytics is active, Google may collect:

  • Pages visited and links clicked
  • Approximate geographic location (country / region level)
  • Browser and device type
  • Referral source (how you arrived at the site)

This data is aggregated and anonymous. It is used solely to understand which releases and content resonate with the audience. No data is sold, shared with third parties for advertising, or used to build individual profiles.

Google's own privacy practices are governed by the Google Privacy Policy.

Cookies

The only first-party cookie this site stores is dh_cookie_consent — a single value (accepted / declined) that remembers your analytics preference. No tracking cookies are set before you make a choice. If you click Decline, no analytics cookies are ever written.

Google Analytics sets its own cookies only after consent is given. You can clear all cookies by clearing your browser's site data for dysonhope.com.

Contact forms and email

The contact section links directly to email addresses (booking@dysonhope.com and studio@dysonhope.com). No form submissions are stored on this server — email goes directly to the artist's inbox and is handled under normal email privacy expectations.

Third-party embeds and links

This site links to external platforms (Spotify, SoundCloud, Beatport, YouTube, Bandcamp, Instagram, Facebook). Following those links takes you off this site and into each platform's own privacy environment. This policy does not cover third-party platforms.

Data retention

No personal data is stored on this site's servers. Analytics data retained by Google follows Google's standard retention settings (default: 14 months). You can request deletion via Google's data controls.

Your choices

You can withdraw analytics consent at any time by clearing your browser's local storage for dysonhope.com — the consent bar will reappear on your next visit, and you can choose Decline. Alternatively, use your browser's built-in privacy controls or a content blocker to prevent analytics loading.
