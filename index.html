About

Three Decades / Underground
DYSON HOPE — PRODUCER / DJ

Shaped by three decades inside underground dance culture — late-night FM radio, LA warehouses, afterhours rooms, European floors, and modern festival systems.

His sound moves through techno, house, breakbeats, and bass music with a focus on hypnotic groove design, low-end pressure, and functional records built for physical spaces — direct, atmospheric, body-first.

Founder of SIGIL.ZERO Records, an independent imprint dedicated to disciplined, DJ-first underground releases.
